Old-Fashioned Lemon Bars

  1. 2 c. all-purpose flour
  2. 1 tsp baking soda
  3. 1 tsp. ground cinnamon
  4. 1/2 tsp. ground nutment
  5. 1/2 tsp. table salt
  6. 3/4 c. shortening
  7. 1 1/2 c. packed brown sugar
  8. 3 Tbsp. fresh lemon juice
  9. 3 Tbsp. lemon zest
  10. 1 c. raisins

Preheat overn to 350F/175C. Lightly grease a 9x9 inch baking pan.
Combine floud, baking soda, spices and salt:set aside. In a large bow, cream together shortening and brown sugar. Beat in the eggs one at a time.
Add in the lemon juice and zest. Gradually blend in the dry ingredients. Stir in raisins.
Spread batter evenly in the prepared baking pan.
Bake 25-30 minutes until lightly colored on top. Cool in pan on wire rack. May be drizzled with a simple confectioner's sugar icing. Cut into bars.
